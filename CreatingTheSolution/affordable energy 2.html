Brief Introduction

Affordable and Clean Energy has been steadily increasing over the past few decasedes and the problem arised with it has been even more concerning. While more and more people are dependant on energy, a large problem is how this energy is being produced and if it is renewable, clean and affordable. Lots of energy is produced by the bruning of fossil fuels, and this is clearly not the cleanest solution. These solutions include: Wave Energy, Biofuels, Geotermal Power, Wind Energy and Tidal Energy. One of the leading cities to induce renewable energy methods is British Columbia. They have set an ambitious EV charging infrastructure strategy, enacted a Zero Emissions Building Plan and have agreed to expand its Neighbourhood Energy Utility.
